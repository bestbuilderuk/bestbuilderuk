Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about building, extensions, and renovations with AB Construction UK Ltd in Swadlincote and South Derbyshire.

1. What areas do you cover?

We are based in Swadlincote and cover a wide radius across South Derbyshire and surrounding areas. Our primary service areas include Swadlincote, Burton-on-Trent, Derby, Ashby-de-la-Zouch, Melbourne, Repton, Woodville, Newhall, Church Gresley, Hartshorne, and Moira.

2. Do you offer free quotes?

Yes, absolutely. We provide completely free, no-obligation site visits and detailed written quotations for all prospective projects, regardless of size. Call us on 01283 310115 to arrange a visit.

3. How much does a house extension cost in Swadlincote?

As a rough guide for 2026, a standard single-storey extension in the Swadlincote area typically starts from around £1,800 to £2,500 per square metre. Double-storey extensions offer better value per square metre. Costs vary based on ground conditions, structural requirements, and your chosen finish.

4. Do I need planning permission for my extension?

Many extensions fall under 'Permitted Development Rights' and do not require formal planning permission, provided they meet specific size and boundary criteria. However, if your property is listed, in a conservation area, or exceeds these limits, you will need planning permission from South Derbyshire District Council. We can advise you on this during our initial visit.

5. How long does a loft conversion take?

A standard Velux loft conversion usually takes 4-6 weeks. A more complex dormer or hip-to-gable conversion typically takes 6-8 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline before work begins and strive to minimise disruption to your daily life.

6. Are you fully insured?

Yes, AB Construction UK Ltd holds comprehensive Public Liability and Employer's Liability insurance. We are fully covered for all types of domestic and commercial construction work, giving you complete peace of mind.

7. Do you manage the whole project from start to finish?

Yes, we offer a complete turnkey service. From initial groundworks and bricklaying to plumbing, electrics, plastering, and final decoration, we manage all trades in-house. You will have a single point of contact throughout the entire build.

8. Can you help with architectural drawings and structural calculations?

While we are primarily builders, we work closely with several trusted local architects and structural engineers in Derbyshire. We can recommend professionals to draw up your plans and handle the Building Control submissions on your behalf.

9. What is the process for a kitchen renovation?

Our kitchen renovation process starts with a consultation to understand your needs. We can either supply and fit a kitchen from our trusted trade suppliers (often at a discount), or we can provide a 'fit-only' service if you have already purchased your kitchen from retailers like Howdens, Wren, or Magnet. We handle all plumbing, electrics, tiling, and flooring.

10. Do you guarantee your work?

Yes, we take immense pride in our craftsmanship. All our structural work comes with a comprehensive guarantee, and we ensure all installations (like boilers or electrical consumer units) are registered for their respective manufacturer warranties.

11. How messy will the building work be?

Construction is inherently messy, but we go to great lengths to protect your home. We use dust sheets, carpet protectors, and seal off working areas where possible. Our team cleans up the site at the end of every working day to ensure your home remains as habitable as possible.

12. Do you undertake commercial building work?

While our primary focus is domestic residential projects (extensions, lofts, renovations), we do undertake select commercial works such as office refurbishments, shop fitting, and commercial brickwork in the Swadlincote and Burton areas.

13. What are your payment terms?

We operate on a transparent, staged payment schedule. For larger projects like extensions, we typically require a small deposit to secure the booking and cover initial materials, followed by staged payments tied to specific project milestones (e.g., foundations poured, roof on, plastering complete). We never ask for the full amount upfront.

14. How soon can you start my project?

Our lead times vary depending on the time of year and the size of your project. Typically, for large projects like extensions, we book 3-6 months in advance. For smaller jobs like brickwork repairs or bathroom fitting, we can often fit you in much sooner. Contact us to check our current availability.

15. Do you handle Building Control inspections?

Yes. For projects requiring Building Regulations approval (like extensions, loft conversions, or structural wall removals), we liaise directly with the local Building Control officer or an approved private inspector. We arrange all necessary site visits to ensure the work is signed off and you receive your final completion certificate.
