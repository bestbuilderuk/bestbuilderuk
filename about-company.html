About AB Construction UK Ltd

Building Dreams, Delivering Quality across Swadlincote and South Derbyshire.

Company Overview

AB Construction UK Ltd is a premier, family-run building and construction company based in Swadlincote, Derbyshire. Founded on the principles of exceptional craftsmanship, transparent pricing, and unwavering reliability, we have established ourselves as one of the most trusted names in the local construction industry.

With over 15 years of collective experience in the trade, our team of skilled professionals manages everything from minor brickwork repairs to large-scale double-storey home extensions and full property renovations. We pride ourselves on offering a complete, fully managed service, taking the stress out of home improvement for our clients.

Our Core Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of construction services, allowing us to act as the sole contractor for complex projects. Our core services include:

  • Home Extensions: Single-storey, double-storey, side returns, and wraparound extensions designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing property.
  • Loft Conversions: Dormer, Velux, and hip-to-gable conversions that unlock the hidden potential of your home's roof space.
  • Brickwork & Masonry: Expert bricklaying, blockwork, repointing, and structural alterations.
  • Bespoke Joinery: Custom timber framing, staircases, doors, windows, and fitted furniture.
  • Kitchen & Bathroom Renovations: Complete design, supply, and installation services, including all plumbing, electrics, and tiling.
  • Full Property Renovations: Comprehensive refurbishment of dilapidated or outdated properties.

Our Service Area

While our headquarters are located in Swadlincote, our service area extends across a wide radius of the Midlands. We regularly undertake projects in:

  • Derbyshire: Swadlincote, Derby, Melbourne, Repton, Woodville, Newhall, Church Gresley, Hartshorne, Overseal, and Castle Gresley.
  • Staffordshire: Burton-on-Trent and surrounding villages.
  • Leicestershire: Ashby-de-la-Zouch, Moira, and the National Forest area.

Our Commitment to Quality and Trust

At AB Construction UK Ltd, we understand that inviting builders into your home requires a significant amount of trust. That is why we operate with complete transparency. We provide detailed, itemised quotations with no hidden costs. We are fully insured with comprehensive Public and Employer's Liability coverage.

We strictly adhere to all current UK Building Regulations and work closely with local Building Control officers to ensure every project is signed off correctly. Our commitment to quality is reflected in our numerous 5-star reviews from satisfied customers across the region.
