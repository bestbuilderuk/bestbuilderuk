AB Construction UK Ltd provides premium bathroom fitting and renovation services across South Derbyshire, Swadlincote, and Burton on Trent.

A Complete Bathroom Renovation Service

A successful bathroom renovation requires the seamless coordination of multiple trades: plumbing, electrical work, plastering, tiling, and joinery. Managing this yourself can be a nightmare. At AB Construction UK Ltd, we provide a fully managed, end-to-end service.

Our comprehensive bathroom fitting service includes:

  • Complete Strip Out: Safe removal and eco-friendly disposal of your old bathroom suite, tiles, and flooring.
  • First Fix Plumbing & Electrics: Rerouting pipes for new layouts, installing concealed shower valves, and fitting safe, zone-compliant electrical lighting and extractor fans.
  • Plastering & Preparation: Ensuring walls are perfectly smooth and waterproofed (tanked) where necessary, particularly in shower enclosures and wet rooms.
  • Professional Tiling: Our expert tilers work with all materials, including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, and mosaics, delivering perfect lines and flawless grouting.
  • Suite Installation: Precision fitting of baths, shower enclosures, toilets, basins, and vanity units.
  • Finishing Touches: Installation of heated towel rails, mirrors, underfloor heating, and final decorating.

Bathroom Styles We Install

We cater to all tastes and budgets, helping you create the perfect space:

Luxury Family Bathrooms

Durable, practical, yet beautiful spaces designed to withstand the rigours of daily family life while providing a touch of luxury. We can incorporate double vanities, large freestanding baths, and smart storage solutions.

Modern Wet Rooms

Sleek, contemporary, and highly accessible. Wet rooms require specialist knowledge to ensure perfect drainage and waterproofing. Our team has extensive experience in creating flawless, leak-free wet rooms that add a real "wow" factor to your home.

En-Suites and Cloakrooms

Maximising space in smaller rooms requires clever design. We can transform a small bedroom corner or under-stairs cupboard into a highly functional and stylish en-suite or downstairs toilet.
