If you're feeling squeezed for space but love your current location, a house extension in Swadlincote is often the perfect solution. Why Choose a House Extension in Swadlincote?

Swadlincote and the surrounding South Derbyshire area offer a fantastic quality of life. Rather than dealing with the stress, stamp duty, and legal fees associated with moving, extending your current home allows you to tailor your living space exactly to your family's needs.

Whether you're dreaming of a spacious open-plan kitchen and dining area, a dedicated home office, or an additional bedroom, a well-designed extension can completely change how you interact with your home.

Types of House Extensions We Build

As experienced local builders, we handle all types of extensions:

  • Single-Storey Rear Extensions: Perfect for creating large, open-plan kitchen/diners that flow seamlessly into your garden.
  • Side Return Extensions: An excellent way to utilise dead space at the side of your property, often used to widen a narrow kitchen.
  • Double-Storey Extensions: Maximise your investment by adding living space downstairs and additional bedrooms or bathrooms upstairs.
  • Wraparound Extensions: Combining a rear and side extension for maximum space creation.

Do I Need Planning Permission in South Derbyshire?

Many house extensions in Swadlincote fall under 'Permitted Development Rights', meaning you won't need formal planning permission, provided your project meets specific criteria regarding size and placement.

However, if your property is listed, located in a conservation area, or if your planned extension exceeds permitted development limits, you will need to apply for planning permission through the South Derbyshire District Council. At AB Construction UK Ltd, we can guide you through this entire process, working alongside local architects to ensure your plans are approved smoothly.

How Much Does a House Extension Cost?

Costs can vary significantly based on the size, complexity, and finish of your project. As a rough guide for 2026, a standard single-storey extension in the Swadlincote area typically starts from around £1,800 to £2,500 per square metre.

We pride ourselves on providing transparent, detailed quotations with no hidden surprises. When you request a quote from us, we break down every element of the build so you know exactly what you're paying for.
