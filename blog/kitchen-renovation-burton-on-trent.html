Kitchen Renovation Burton on Trent: Creating Your Dream Space

Home / Blog / Article
📅 Published: April 2026 | 👤 Author: AB Construction Ltd | ⏱️ 4 min read

They say the kitchen is the heart of the home, and at AB Construction Ltd, we couldn't agree more. A well-designed kitchen isn't just a place to cook; it's a place to gather, entertain, and live. If you're looking for a top-quality kitchen renovation in Burton on Trent, Swadlincote, or South Derbyshire, our expert team is here to bring your vision to life.

Why Invest in a Kitchen Renovation?

An outdated, poorly laid-out kitchen can make daily tasks frustrating. A professional renovation offers:

  • Improved Functionality: We design kitchens that work for you, optimising the "working triangle" (sink, fridge, cooker) for maximum efficiency.
  • Increased Storage: Modern cabinetry solutions, from deep pan drawers to clever corner pull-outs, ensure every inch of space is utilised.
  • Enhanced Aesthetics: Whether you prefer a sleek, handleless modern design or a classic shaker-style traditional kitchen, we deliver a flawless finish.
  • Added Property Value: A high-quality kitchen is one of the most sought-after features for homebuyers, making it a solid investment in your property's future.

Our Comprehensive Kitchen Fitting Service

Unlike some companies that only supply the cabinets, AB Construction Ltd provides a complete, end-to-end kitchen renovation service in the Burton on Trent area. This means you only have to deal with one trusted contractor.

1. Strip Out and Preparation

We safely remove and dispose of your old kitchen. If your new design requires structural changes—such as knocking down a wall to create an open-plan kitchen/diner—our experienced builders handle all the structural work safely and cleanly.

2. Plumbing and Electrics

Our qualified tradespeople will reroute plumbing for new sink positions, install gas lines for new hobs, and completely rewire the space to accommodate modern appliances, under-cabinet lighting, and ample plug sockets.

3. Plastering and Flooring

We ensure perfectly smooth walls ready for decorating or tiling. We also install all types of flooring, from durable luxury vinyl tiles (LVT) to classic ceramic or porcelain tiles.

4. Expert Installation

Our master joiners meticulously install your chosen cabinetry, ensuring perfect alignment and a flawless finish. We expertly fit all worktops, whether laminate, solid wood, quartz, or granite.

The Open-Plan Trend

One of the most popular requests we receive in Burton on Trent and Swadlincote is for open-plan living. By removing the wall between a cramped kitchen and an underused dining room, we can create a stunning, light-filled space that becomes the focal point of family life. We handle all the necessary structural calculations and steel beam installations required for these transformative projects.
