Luxury bathroom design, fitting, and refurbishment across Swadlincote and South Derbyshire.

Your bathroom should be a place of relaxation and comfort. At AB Construction UK Ltd, we design and install stunning bathrooms that combine style with functionality. Whether you're looking for a complete bathroom renovation, a new en-suite, or a modern wet room, our skilled team delivers exceptional results across Swadlincote and the surrounding areas.

  • Bathroom refurbishment and renovation

Luxury Finishes, Practical Design

A great bathroom balances beautiful aesthetics with practical design. We help you choose the right layout, fixtures, and finishes to create a bathroom that looks stunning and works perfectly for your daily routine.

From rainfall showers and freestanding baths to heated towel rails and underfloor heating, we can incorporate all the luxury features you desire. We also specialise in clever storage solutions to keep your bathroom clutter-free.

Expert Tiling & Finishing

Tiling is one of the most important elements of any bathroom, and our tilers are true craftsmen. Whether you choose large format porcelain, natural stone, or intricate mosaic tiles, we ensure perfect cuts, consistent grout lines, and a waterproof finish that will last for years.

Every bathroom we complete is fully waterproofed, properly ventilated, and finished to the highest standard. We don't cut corners — because in a bathroom, quality matters.

Comprehensive Service Offerings

  • Comprehensive trade management (Plumbing, Electrics, Tiling, Plastering)

  • Workmanship guarantees and product warranties
