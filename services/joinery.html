🪚 Joinery

Custom woodwork and carpentry solutions across Swadlincote and South Derbyshire.

Home / Services / Joinery

AB Construction UK Ltd offers expert joinery services that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. Our skilled joiners create beautiful, functional woodwork for homes and businesses across Swadlincote and the surrounding areas. From custom staircases to fitted wardrobes, we bring your vision to life in wood.

Our Joinery Services

Our comprehensive joinery services cover every aspect of woodwork and carpentry:

  • Bespoke staircases and handrails
  • Internal and external doors — supply and fit
  • Window installation and replacement
  • Fitted wardrobes and storage solutions
  • Custom shelving and cabinetry
  • Skirting boards, architraves, and coving
  • Decking and outdoor woodwork
  • Structural timber work — roofs, floors, and frames
  • Loft hatches and boarding

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

Great joinery is about more than just cutting and fitting wood — it's about understanding the material, the space, and the client's vision. Our joiners bring years of experience and a genuine passion for their craft to every project.

Whether you're looking for a stunning new staircase to become the centrepiece of your home, or practical storage solutions that maximise your space, we deliver results that exceed expectations. We use quality timber and materials, and our attention to detail ensures a perfect finish every time.

We also handle all structural timber work, including roof construction, floor joists, and timber framing for extensions and new builds. Whatever your joinery needs, AB Construction UK Ltd has the skills and experience to deliver.
