At AB Construction Ltd, our skilled bricklayers deliver exceptional brickwork for residential and commercial projects across Swadlincote and the wider Derbyshire area. Whether you need a new garden wall, structural brickwork for an extension, or repairs to existing masonry, our team has the expertise and experience to deliver outstanding results.

Our Brickwork Services

We provide a comprehensive range of brickwork services, all delivered to the highest standards of craftsmanship:

  • New build brickwork and blockwork
  • Extension brickwork — single and double storey
  • Garden walls and boundary walls
  • Brick repairs and repointing
  • Decorative brickwork and feature walls
  • Stonework and natural stone walling
  • Chimney repairs and rebuilds
  • Retaining walls and structural brickwork

Why Choose Our Brickwork Services?

Brickwork is the foundation of any great building, and at AB Construction Ltd, we take immense pride in every course we lay. Our bricklayers are time-served professionals who understand that precision, consistency, and attention to detail are what separate good brickwork from great brickwork.

We work with all types of bricks, blocks, and natural stone, and we're happy to match existing brickwork for extensions and repairs. Our team ensures clean, straight lines, consistent mortar joints, and a finish that will stand the test of time.

Every brickwork project we undertake is backed by our commitment to quality materials, skilled workmanship, and transparent pricing. We provide detailed quotes before any work begins, so you know exactly what to expect.
