🍳 Kitchens

Complete kitchen design and installation across Swadlincote and South Derbyshire.

Home / Services / Kitchens

The kitchen is the heart of every home, and at AB Construction Ltd, we create kitchens that are as beautiful as they are functional. From contemporary handleless designs to classic shaker styles, our team designs and installs kitchens that perfectly suit your lifestyle, taste, and budget. Based in Swadlincote, we serve clients across Derbyshire and beyond.

Our Kitchen Services

We offer a complete kitchen service from initial design to final installation:

  • Full kitchen design and planning
  • Kitchen removal and disposal
  • Complete kitchen installation
  • Worktop supply and fitting — laminate, granite, quartz
  • Tiling — walls and floors
  • Plumbing and gas work
  • Electrical work — lighting, sockets, appliances
  • Plastering and decorating
  • Flooring installation
  • Kitchen extensions for larger spaces

Design Your Perfect Kitchen

Every kitchen we install starts with understanding how you use your kitchen. Do you love cooking and need maximum worktop space? Do you entertain and want an open-plan kitchen-diner? Do you need clever storage solutions for a smaller space?

We work with you to design a kitchen that works perfectly for your needs. We can work with kitchen units from any supplier, or recommend trusted suppliers who offer excellent quality at competitive prices.

Quality Installation

A kitchen is only as good as its installation, and our fitters are meticulous in their work. Every unit is perfectly level, every joint is tight, and every detail is finished to perfection. We coordinate all trades — plumbing, electrics, tiling, and decorating — so your new kitchen is completed efficiently and to the highest standard.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a kitchen renovation take?

A standard kitchen renovation typically takes 2–4 weeks from strip-out to completion. If structural work is involved, such as removing a wall to create an open-plan layout, the project may take 4–6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline before work begins and keep disruption to a minimum.
