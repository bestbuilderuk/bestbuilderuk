Types of Loft Conversions

We offer all types of loft conversions to suit your property and requirements:

  • Dormer loft conversions — the most popular choice
  • Hip-to-gable conversions — ideal for semi-detached and detached homes
  • Velux/rooflight conversions — the most cost-effective option
  • L-shaped loft conversions — maximum space for larger properties
  • Mansard loft conversions — for maximum headroom
  • Bungalow loft conversions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need planning permission for a loft conversion in Swadlincote?

Most loft conversions in Swadlincote fall under Permitted Development Rights and do not require planning permission, provided the additional volume does not exceed 40 cubic metres for terraced houses or 50 cubic metres for detached and semi-detached houses. However, dormer conversions visible from the front of the property, or properties in conservation areas, may require planning permission. AB Construction UK Ltd handles all planning enquiries on your behalf.

How much does a loft conversion cost in Derbyshire?

Loft conversion costs in Derbyshire typically range from £25,000 to £50,000 depending on the type. A basic Velux conversion starts from around £25,000, a dormer conversion from £35,000, and a hip-to-gable conversion from £45,000. These prices include all structural work, insulation, plastering, electrics, and a staircase. Contact us for a free, personalised quote.
