🏠 Extensions

Transform your home with a beautifully designed and expertly built extension in Swadlincote.

Home / Services / Extensions

A home extension is one of the best investments you can make — adding valuable living space and significantly increasing your property's value. At AB Construction Ltd, we specialise in designing and building extensions of all sizes across Swadlincote and the surrounding areas. From initial concept to final handover, we manage every aspect of your extension project.

Types of Extensions We Build

We design and build a wide range of home extensions to suit every property and budget:

  • Single-storey rear extensions
  • Double-storey extensions
  • Side return extensions
  • Wraparound extensions
  • Kitchen extensions
  • Garage conversions
  • Orangeries and garden rooms
  • Permitted development extensions

Full Project Management

Building an extension involves many moving parts — from planning permission and building regulations to groundwork, brickwork, roofing, electrics, plumbing, and finishing. At AB Construction Ltd, we manage the entire process so you don't have to worry about a thing.

We work with trusted architects, structural engineers, and building control officers to ensure your extension meets all regulations and is built to the highest standards. Our team coordinates all trades, manages timelines, and keeps you informed at every stage.

Add Value to Your Home

A well-designed extension can add 15-20% to your property's value while giving you the extra space you need. Whether you want a larger kitchen, an additional bedroom, or a spacious living area, we'll help you make the most of your home.

We'll discuss your ideas, provide expert advice on what's achievable within your budget, and deliver a finished extension that seamlessly blends with your existing property.
